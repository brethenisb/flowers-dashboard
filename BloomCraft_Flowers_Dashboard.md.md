# 🌸 BloomCraft Flowers Dashboard

## 📌 Business Name
**BloomCraft Flowers**

---

## 🌼 Business Overview

BloomCraft Flowers is a modern flower and bouquet business specializing in fresh floral arrangements, customized bouquets, floral gifts, event decorations, wedding flower arrangements, and same-day flower delivery services. The business aims to provide elegant floral solutions for every occasion while delivering exceptional customer experiences.

---

## 🎯 Business Goals

- Simplify flower ordering and customization.
- Manage customer orders efficiently.
- Track flower inventory in real time.
- Improve delivery management.
- Monitor business performance through analytics.
- Enhance customer satisfaction through feedback and reviews.
